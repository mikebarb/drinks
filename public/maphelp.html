Help for the Map Page

When you initially load this map, the locations of our BMI churches are displayed.

The buttons at the top centre of the page (excluding 'Help'):

  • allows you to control the display of markers for these ministries.
  • Single click on these buttons will display or hide the locations for that organisation.
  • Double click will display the Web Site for the organisation.

    • For any map marker (showing the location):

  • mouse moved over top of the marker will display the title
  • double click will display the web site providing additional for that location - if available.

    • Anywhere else on the map

  • right click will place a marker on the map and display the latitude and longitude for that location.
  • right clicking on this marker will remove the marker.
